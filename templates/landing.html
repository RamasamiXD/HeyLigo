Hanford Livingston Virgo GEO600 All

Informational Retrieval and Recommendation System built for Gravitational Wave Observatories

Lets one know more about this multi-physics experiment by providing easy access to the efforts carried out to achieve the desired astrophysical sensitivity. This machine learning based interface answers user queries about the instrument by making use of archived activities undertaken at the observatories. The application identifies trending issues at the site and sends out alerts to subscribed users based on their interests.
